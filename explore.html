Welcome to the Explore Page!

The Explore page is a central space where students can discover and connect with others, join conversations, and access useful academic resources.

Explore Features:

  • Search Bar: Find students, study groups, and materials such as notes and past exam papers.
  • Student Profiles: Browse profiles of students in your course or with similar interests, and start chats directly.
  • Active Chat Rooms: Join or create study groups, discussion rooms, and share insights.
  • Resources: Access study materials, summaries, guides, and shared documents, organized by subject or module.
  • Trending Content: Keep up-to-date with the latest discussions, resources, and shared materials.
  • Opportunities: Check out internships, bursaries, and campus events you can apply for.

Get Involved

We encourage participation through options to upload resources, start discussions, and create study groups. Your contributions help build a stronger community!
